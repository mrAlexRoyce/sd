Data Integration

  • Import / Export Studio
    If you have structured data, then you’ll be ready to upload it into SIMBA in no time. SIMBA’s Import/Export Studio allows easy importing directly into or exporting from the platform. If we don’t already have a pre-built connector to your data source, then this feature may be your answer to frequent uploads into SIMBA.
  • Open API
    SIMBA’s robust API allows clients to directly integrate with internal or external systems, data warehouses, and 3rd parties to push or pull data and files to and from SIMBA. We can guide your staff in working with our API or our client success team can build the connections for you.
  • BI / Reporting Integration Options
    SIMBA uses our own integration capabilities to embed Highcharts into the SIMBA interface. These data visualization screens and dashboards can be tailored to your needs beyond what is included out-of-box. Further, using our API and other tools, clients can directly integrate with their own business intelligence and reporting tools for use outside of SIMBA.
Research Management Software

SIMBA Software’s research management solution drives hundreds of GPs’ and LPs’ investment research processes while facilitating operational and corporate due diligence efforts. SIMBA is designed specifically for the complex and often proprietary research approaches by portfolio managers investing across many asset classes.

RMS for Institutions & Asset Allocators


Owners of asset use SIMBA’s research management capabilities to optimize their research and investment processes across multiple asset classes. SIMBA drives LPs’ research on: traditional/publicly traded securities and funds, alternative funds, direct private deals, and hard assets.

SIMBA has specific reporting and interfaces for fund manager tracking and due diligence, allowing you to combine operational reviews with investment and quantitative measures. Management company profiles are linked to the funds and other products they offer to eliminate data and document redundancy and simplify reporting. Historical fund performance and exposures are captured to give you a 360-degree view and enable robust manager reports and tear sheets. Combined with existing portfolio data, SIMBA’s what-if reporting shows the potential impacts on overall portfolio exposures.

Global ODD teams leverage SIMBA’s flexible interface and entity structure to construct their processes as they see fit, using their own terminology. SIMBA’s robust relational structure allows you to centralize service provider due diligence with links to the managers and funds that use their services.

Native integration with Outlook® and the Microsoft Office® suite, paired with the SIMBA Exchange rules engine and workflow management features, help expedite due diligence and ongoing monitoring of portfolio managers, direct deals, co-investments, and even vendors by automatically linking and routing imported data and communications to relevant profiles.
