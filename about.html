About SIMBA

SIMBA Electronics Trading offers an industry-tailored, highly configurable platform that solves challenges across the private investment landscape for fund managers (GPs), deal teams, asset owners / allocators (LPs), and related industry participants.

The software platform benefits from intensive collaboration with internationally prominent clients to optimize the front-to-back office productivity of deal teams, fund managers and asset allocators, while providing innovative solutions to facilitate data exchange between these firms.
